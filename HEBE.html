Problem Statement

My client Hebe is a women's clothing boutique located in New Zealand that caters to women in their prime. I am re-designed Hebe’s website to be more feasible and to look more fun, credible, and visually appealing to the audience. An aeasthetically pleasing design improves user experience in by creating a positive response in user’s brains; leading them to believe the design works better.

Define

Competitors

Understanding the landscape is key to a successful eCommerce redesign. I've conducted a comprehensive analysis of industry competitors to ensure the new website not only meets but exceeds market standards.

Luca + Grae

Luca + Grae is an online boutique created by YouTuber, Aspyn Ovard Ferris in 2016. Her selected clothing is inspired by her many world travels to places such as Paris and Bali. Their brand style is described as modern and feminine, yet trendy and comfortable.


Pros

They have a large social media presence. They are very active/ up to date online. The cost of their clothing is more affordable.

Cons

Their clothes are more youthful/casual than Hebe. Doesn’t match their brand description. They do not have a stand alone store- online only. Their website footer text is too light to read.

Whimsy + Row

Whimsy + Row is an eco-conscious lifestyle brand from Los Angeles, California. They offer quality goods and sustainable clothing. Their mission has been to provide ease and elegance for the modern, sustainable woman.


Pros

They make eco-conscious clothing. Online reviews are positive with 4.5 stars on Yelp. Unique selection of clothes and accessories.

Cons

Doesn’t have too many Google reviews. They are not taking returns at this time. They don’t have a shopping option on their Instagram.

Beginning Boutique

Beginniing Boutique is a team of over 80 dedicated people who aren’t afraid to dream big and we sure can pack one hell of a punch! Their team is committed to giving up-to-the-minute trends and styles as soon as they drop, with super fast delivery worldwide and above and beyond customer service that goes the extra mile.


Pros

They recycle and upscycle Their packaging is used with bidegradable materials. They have a slection of clothing that is on trend. They Have a shopping option on their Instagram and have 1 miliiion followers. Have many reviews and clothing hauls on Youtube.

Cons

Doesn’t have Google reviews. Have a rating of 3 stars on trust pilot and a 3.8 on trust pilot and 3 stars on product review. Its has no reviews on Yelp. Many complaints on shipping and returns.
