Table of Contents Resdesign


layout

I redesigned and created a typography focused layout for a table of contents pagespread utilizing layout and design principles.


Tasks

To create varying compositions using elements of given type, to practice applying layout principles such as type Hierarchy, negative space, and grids to designs, to practice analytical skills and use of design principles and strategies, and to utilize good craftmanship to create the finished compositions.


Problem Statement

For this project, I redesigned the table of contents for a uta publication. Parts of the table of contents were distracting, crowded, hard to comprehend and there was not much consistency in the design. I did reserch on current design trends, layout principles and design elements to create a more efficient minal and aesthetically pleasing design. In this process book Ii will be sharing my reaserch, inpirsation and design process.

Reflection

Overall, I enjoyed redesigning the table of contents. I learned about different design trends and keeping balance within the layout. It was challenging because, It was difficult for me to come up with a design that showcases all the information while being bold and minimal. It was also my first time using inDesign. After, doing plenty research and learning InDesign tools I ended up with a design that I think exhibits the contents well without being overbearng.

During my digital process, I was indecisive about my design. My first layout was crowded, did not utilze white space well and there was no flow. I altered it many times and came up with a completly different idea. I decided to blow up the volume roman numeral, added contents around it in small text, a pop of color and a grey section to give it asymmetry.

I think my layout could be improved by better placing of the contents I was also unsure if I should leave the backround plain white or maybe placing the grey behind the roman numerals.
