Sketches

These sketches are quick visual representations of initial ideas and concepts before more detailed work begins. This stage allows designers to explore various possibilities, experiment with layouts, and communicate basic design elements. Sketches help in refining and iterating design concepts efficiently, providing a foundation for the more polished stages of the project. This phase is crucial for brainstorming, collaboration, and ensuring that the design aligns with the project's objectives before moving on to more detailed and refined design iterations.

Final Animations

These final vector illustration animations represent the culmination of creative ideation, design refinement, and technical execution, ready for presentation or implementation in the desired context.

Reflection

Creating animations for Parent Source was a dynamic and purposeful endeavor. It involves blending creativity with strategic communication to effectively reach the target audience.

Throughout the process, the key considerations include aligning visuals with the rebranded image, conveying the emotional journey of parenthood, and ensuring inclusivity. The animations serve as a powerful tool to not only refresh the brand's visual identity but also to engage and resonate with the diverse experiences of expectant parents.

Collaboration, feedback, and a thoughtful launch strategy contribute to the success of these animations in capturing attention, conveying the brand's messages, and fostering a connection with the company's mission.
